What is TrialGuard Pro?

TrialGuard Pro is a Chrome Extension that monitors your trial signups, provides smart countdown alerts, and archives your cancellation proof—all in one place.

  • Unlimited Tracking: Keep tabs on every trial.
  • Smart Alerts: Choose exactly when you want to be reminded.
  • Proof of Cancel: Store confirmation numbers and notes.
